More about Instant QR

Instant QR is a fast, lightweight, and intuitive web application that allows users to generate QR codes for websites in seconds. Designed with simplicity and performance in mind, Instant QR eliminates unnecessary steps and delivers QR codes instantly — no login required.

Whether you're a student, developer, business owner, or marketer, Instant QR helps you convert website links into scannable QR codes effortlessly.

Key Features

  • Instant Generation: Generate QR codes immediately.
  • Smart URL Detection: Supports .com, .edu, .in, .org, etc.
  • Multiple QR Support: Generate multiple QRs at once.
  • Clean UI: Professional startup-style interface.
  • No Login Required: Free and open for everyone.
